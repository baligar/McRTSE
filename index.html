McRTSE: MULTI-CHANNEL REVERBERANT TARGET SOUND EXTRACTION

Author:

About: We address the challenge of target sound extraction and detection in reverberant environments for general sound classes. We highlight the limitations of existing approaches when applied to reverberant conditions. In response, we develop an end-to-end Multi-channel Reverberant Target Sound Extraction (McRTSE) model, that outperforms current methods in these conditions. Recognizing the need for realistic training datasets, we introduce the rNIGENS and rNIGENS-XL datasets, which reflect realistic reverberant mixtures. We evaluate McRTSE extensively and show its robustness in handling challenging reverberant audio soundscapes.
